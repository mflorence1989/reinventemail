Reinvent University Now Online

We’re proud to announce the launch of Reinvent University, our new learning portal. Beginning Nov. 1, technicians will be pre-registered and able to log in to our online portal to access on-demand training programs at www.reinventuniversity.com.

Initially, this includes technical training on the most commonly requested support topics on Metaswitch and Edgewater products, such as:
  • Building SIP Trunking
  • Building a Business Group & Associated Seats
  • Handling Calls to a Primary Number
  • Using Force LNP Look Up
  • How Administrators Control a Business Group
  • Installation & Deployment of EdgeMarc with Reinvent
  More courses on Sales & Marketing will be available in the coming months.

Reinvent Partner Portal Enhancements

We’ve updated functionality in the Reinvent Partner Portal (RPP) to simplify and speed up your ability to sell and service your clients.

RPP enhancements include the ability to:
  • Request a simulated signature for proposals generated through QuoteLink
  • Complete a Wholesale Order in a single-page form
  • Search for line items to add to the Proposal Form and the Private-Label Billing Order Form
  • Directly update line options, including CNAM, directory listings, call forwarding, and texting to new and ported numbers without having to call into customer service
  • Define retail products by main number, device or peripheral as well as a phone number
For more information, on each of these upgrades, please download our latest Sales Bulletin from RPP.
